About Me »

Much can be said of newness, and for me, almost everything is now new.  I am newly unsettled, unattached, and yes, unemployed (though of my choosing), and with this new-found newness I intend to travel the world - but to do so at a pace that allows me to really understand and appreciate the places that I am visiting.  The itinerary is fairly set: Latin America from now until Christmas, then India, Australia and China over the first four months of 2012.  The first bit I'm doing solo, but for the second half I will be joined by different friends for different stages of the journey.  My goals are, jokingly, to not die and to have fun, in that order; however I would expand them to also include taking time to look beyond the biggest tourist attraction, the brightest light, or the loudest sound, and to discover, if possible, what universality exists among the people living in all the diverse places I travel to.  I also have every wish to meet and truly connect with both fellow travelers and locals, and with luck to germinate some friendships that will last beyond these months of adventure. 

To you the friend or anonymous reader, I welcome you as a companion on this journey!  I am also new to blogging and new to website management, so this will be a work in progress, one could say much like life itself.  Feedback and commentary are always welcome.  Again, welcome and cheers.

October 6, 2011
